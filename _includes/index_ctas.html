features you'll love

privacy by default

We take privacy seriously. All content posted to Graft is encrypted in transit and again at rest.

linkable notes

Any two notes within a project can be linked to highlight related ideas.

no note limits

Graft doesn't put a limit on your knowledge. Create as many notes as you need.

collaborate with your team

Graft includes the collaborative tools needed to help your team cultivate new knowledge.

impact score

Graft analyzes the relationships between notes to measure how important they are to the network.
